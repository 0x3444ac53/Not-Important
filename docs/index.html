Being me is kind of like reading a sequel to the voynich manuscript, and the entire time there is a crushing feeling of agnosthesia, references to books I've never read and a lot of meandering about midding. There is a trend toward altschmerz somewhere in the middle, and a longing for flashovers that may never come. The liberosis is palatable. With this last chapter of my life closing there is a focus on adronitis, and a desperation towards creating a new rigor samsa knowing this will only exacerbate the disconnected sonder I'm prone to. I do what I can to submit myself to the mortifying ordeal, and yet whenever I have, I've only felt mortified and never known. I can tell people I am in my 1989 era, or my modern philosophy era, but I am neither a nightmare dressed like a daydream nor a thinking thing that thinks. To my mind,, I am the collective efforts of the ancestors, and a mosaic of all the ideas I have ever encountered. Why should I pretend to be original, when all I am is these ideas that belong to other people?

Is it a test? is that all? am I making this entire thing because I just really really really need someone, anyone to be willing to understand, but I cannot give that freely.
I don't want to tell people that I like an artist called Samsa and that I listen to Butterflies on repeat whenever I feel like I'm falling in love. I want to tell them that I would love to be haunted through my screen and pray that they know what it is I am trying to say.

I don't know
